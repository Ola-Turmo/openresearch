# Examples

These examples are safe, compact transformations. They show how to preserve the user's idea while adding GPT Image 2 structure for Codex-native generation.

## Example 1: candid pet meme

User brief:

```text
snapchat meme of a dog sitting on grass
```

Codex-ready prompt:

```text
Generate a realistic candid phone photo of one dog sitting on grass in a park, framed like a casual Snapchat meme. The dog is the clear subject, with a slightly imperfect handheld composition and natural daylight from the location. Add a playful casual vibe without adding unrelated people or objects. Use realistic fur detail, ordinary grass texture, and a slightly soft phone-camera look rather than a studio photo. Avoid watermarks, extra logos, artificial background, over-sharpening, and excessive glare. Aspect ratio: 4:3.
```

## Example 2: product hero

User brief:

```text
minimalist ad for a matte black coffee grinder
```

Codex-ready prompt:

```text
Generate a minimalist premium product advertisement for one matte black coffee grinder as the hero subject. Place it centered on a clean stone-gray podium against a soft light-gray gradient background. Use a three-quarter front angle so the grinder silhouette, hopper, dial, and base are visible. Lighting is soft diffused studio light with crisp but gentle shadows and subtle highlights on the matte finish. Add a refined coffee-equipment campaign mood with plenty of negative space for future copy. No visible brand logos, no extra text, no watermark, no unrelated props. Aspect ratio: 1:1.
```

## Example 3: cinematic still

User brief:

```text
retro sci-fi detective walking in the rain
```

Codex-ready prompt:

```text
Generate a cinematic film still of a retro sci-fi detective walking alone through a rain-soaked neon street at night. The subject wears a long dark coat and brimmed hat, seen in a medium-wide three-quarter rear view, with reflections on wet pavement and steam rising from vents. Use practical neon signs, cool blue ambient shadows, warm rim light, subtle film grain, shallow depth of field, and a moody noir color grade. The frame should feel like a natural moment from a movie, not a posed poster. Avoid extra text, watermark, plastic CGI surfaces, and over-saturated neon. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

## Example 4: UI mockup

User brief:

```text
mobile dashboard for plant watering app, cute but premium
```

Codex-ready prompt:

```text
Generate a polished mobile app dashboard mockup for a plant watering app. Use a 9:16 vertical phone-screen composition with clean rounded cards, soft cream background, fresh green accents, and premium friendly typography. Layout includes a header with greeting, a hero card for today's watering schedule, three plant status cards, a weekly moisture chart, and a bottom navigation bar with simple line icons. Use tasteful botanical illustrations and realistic UI spacing. Visible text should be short and readable; avoid random filler text. No watermarks, no unrelated logos, no broken alignment, no distorted icons. Aspect ratio: 9:16.
```

## Example 5: infographic

User brief:

```text
diagram explaining how rainwater harvesting works
```

Codex-ready prompt:

```text
Generate a clean educational infographic explaining how residential rainwater harvesting works. Show a simple house cross-section with rain falling on the roof, gutters directing water into a filter, storage tank, pump, and garden irrigation outlet. Use numbered steps, clear arrows, simple labels, consistent line weight, and a calm blue/green palette. Include enough technical detail to understand the water flow without overcrowding the diagram. Text should be large and readable. Avoid decorative clutter, unlabeled arrows, tiny microtext, watermarks, and random extra systems. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```
